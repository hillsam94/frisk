WHAT IS FRISK CHAT?

Frisk is a social media service based in London, England. It can be accessed from your PC and from various mobile devices. Once a performer has registered on Frisk they are able to run a subscription based social media service. Frisk offers multiple ways for performers to build relationships and provide exclusive content to their subscribers, or "fans".

Frisks' business model mimics other social media platforms like Twitter, Instagram & Facebook. It is popular with influencers in a range of areas such as fashion icons, physical fitness instructors, photographers, independent musicians, chefs and cooks to name a few as well as celebrities and icons. Frisk allows performers and content creators to receive income directly from their fans on a monthly basis as well as on tips and the pay-per-view (PPV) posts and message feature.

The Purpose Of Frisk

A unique feature on Frisk in comparison to other subscription based social media networks is that performers have a free and a paid wall and are able to send PPV messages to all of their fans enabling them to earn good money even from fans that haven't signed up to the subscription service.

Frisk performers are also allocated a personal account manager to help you get started and navigate the site. This is a first in this industry where support has often been criticised as lacking. 

Frisk has been built with the performer and end user in mind. Its user friendly, no fuss platform makes the process of registering, uploading and verification a task most could complete with ease. Add to this the multiple income avenues it makes Frisk a very appealing offer.

Frisk is free to sign up for both performers and a fans. Performers have 2 "walls", a free wall, that users only need register to view, and a paid wall that fans are required to pay a subscription for. This feature is definitely a step forward from other platforms and allows interaction between performers and fans regardless whether the fan has paid or not.

Frisk is a social media platform that enables performers and creators to connect with and interact with their followers and fan base. Due to the subscription service it also allows performers to make money through subscription fee's and pay-per-view content. Frisk performers can create exclusive content for their fans and charge them to view it.

Frisk fans can follow their favourite influencers at no charge and get to see extra content, not seen anywhere else, on the performers "free" wall, try before you buy so to speak. For the ardent fan there is then the option to gain access to premium content only available through the paid subscription option.

Paid subscription social media platforms like Frisk are all the rage at the moment and you will be hard pressed to go onto any mainstream social network without seeing someone advertising their subscription based service. There is very good reason for this, until these types of websites came about influencers were reliant on external companies to monetize their work. Agencies, Google AdSense and sponsorship for example, were some of the main sources of income for a lot of artists, models and influencers. With websites like Frisk the creator controls the content and gets to keep the lions share of the income generated by cutting out the middle man or woman.

It is little wonder so many influencers and artists are flocking to sites like Frisk when you consider the alternative. Lets say a model gets contracted for a photo shoot. The model gets paid a one time fee for the photo's. With Frisk the same model can pay for a photo shoot, keep the images and sell them over and over again through the Frisk platform for as long as fans keep signing up. This can be repeated endlessly creating a constant income stream for the Frisk performer. All of this at no cost to set up. The only outlay is the commission and that is after a sale has been made so that cost has been covered by the fan purchasing the subscription or PPV content.

A final selling point would be that Frisk performer accounts could easily be ran beside any regular work the performer may have. There are no time restraints or issues with things like weather or travel disruptions, Frisk performers can choose what to create, when they want to create it and how much to charge for that content!
